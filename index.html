About Us

The government and NGOs are planning to develop an urban Primary Health Care (PHC) project at the backdrop of gradual phasing out of existing NGOs-based urban clinics. UNICEF came forward and engaged a group of consultants involving Public Health, Health Economics and ICT experts to design the “Model Urban PHC Clinics (Aalo Clinics)” which was later endorsed by the government. .
